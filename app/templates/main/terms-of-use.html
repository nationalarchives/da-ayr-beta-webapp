    Terms of use

    What is in these terms?

    These terms tell you the rules for using the Access Your Records service at https://ayr.nationalarchives.gov.uk.

    By using the Access Your Records service, you accept these terms

    By using the Access Your Records service, you confirm that you accept these terms of use and that you agree to comply with them.

    Use

    Use of Access Your Records must comply with government security policies around the handling of Official and Official-Sensitive information.

    To maintain the security of this service, we may restrict access based on IP or disable user accounts if we identify any breach to our terms of use.

    Coverage

    You should be aware that Access Your Records is not a complete record of all digital records held at The National Archives. AYR currently holds those born-digital records transferred via the Transfer Digital Records service. We are working to develop the service to include born- digital records transferred to The National Archives via other transfer mechanisms.

    You should be aware that some records may subsequently have been opened or closed, or had their descriptions and titles opened or closed. These changes may not be immediately reflected in the Access Your Records service.

    You should satisfy yourself that access to a record complies with all security requirements.

    Formats

    Access Your Records has been designed to provide users with access to transferred digital records in their original formats. The available formats may no longer be compatible to view with modern software.

    For the more popular file types, Access Your Records renders a version of the record for viewing in a browser.

    Who we are and how to contact us

    The Access Your Records service is provided by The National Archives.

    You can contact us by email at digitalarchivingaccess@nationalarchives.gov.uk.

    If your online access is limited and you need to speak to someone you can call us during office hours on 020 8876 3444. Leave your number and message and we will call you back.
